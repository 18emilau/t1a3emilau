MARIANAS TRENCH

This website is about a band called Marianas trench. They have three albums their first album, FIX ME, their second album, MASTERPIECE THEATRE and third album EVER AFTER. Members consist of Josh Ramsay (lead vocalist/ guitar), Matt Webb (lead guitarist), Mike Ayley (bass guitarist), and Ian Casselman (drummer). Their first album FIX ME, came out in 2006, and the band decided while recording they would not use any autotuning or editing. FIX ME released three singles including SHAKE TRAMP which was their third and most successful single. SAY ANYTHING and DECIDED TO BREAK IT were the other singles released from FIX ME.Second album MASTERPIECE THEATRE was released in 2009, the band came prepared with more advertising including a TV commercial and landed 5 singles CROSS MY HEART, BESIDE YOU, ALL TO MYSELF, CELEBRITY STATUS, and GOOD TO YOU. GOOD TO YOU featured Jessica Lee, who won a contest they held to sing with them at a concert, the band later decided they were to make GOOD TO YOU a single, featuring her. Jessica Lee now has an album of her own. Josh Ramsay has also been featured in 2 other songs, HIT ME UP by Danny Fernandez, and SOUR CANDY by Carly Rae Jepsen.
